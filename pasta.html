Ingredents

  • Pasta
  • water
  • stove
  • salt
  • bowl
  • plate
  • fork/spoon
  1. add water to the bowl
  2. add pasta to the bowl
  3. add salt to the bowl
  4. put that bowl on stove
  5. boil for 10 min on low heat
  6. serve it a cup and u r ready to do whatever u want with that boiled pasta
